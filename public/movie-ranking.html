The Best Movies According to Jory

My Top 3 Movies of all Time

The Labyrinth

Jim Henson played a pivotol role in my childhood and Bowie as Jareth rocked my world. I wanted to both be him and experience him.

Batman Returns

I fell in love with Michelle Pfeifer in this movie. It is, hands down, one of the best Batman movies of all time.

Good Will Hunting

An amazing film about adversity, friendship, and healing from childhood trauma.
